## Programme

- :one: [The first commands]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link _posts/2022-03-01-Bash-1.md %}) - Open a terminal and start trying the first commands (like *pwd*, *cd*, *mkdir*, *man*, *wget*) and familiarise with absolute and relative paths.
